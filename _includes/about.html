How does commissioning a portrait work?

We'll be here to guide you through each step, but here's an overview so you know what to expect! ðŸ—º

  • 1. Pick an offering:

    Which package is right for you?

    Peruse our catalog of product offerings or contact us directly for a consultation to discuss which offering is best tailored to your desires, space requirements, or other needs.

  • 2. Book your snapshot session:

    Shaping your visual profile

    Our snapshot session is an appointment where we complete a guided questionnaire together. It captures your personality at a point in time like a photograph, accurately reflecting who you are in that moment, and mapping all your facets to varying percentages of visual characteristics we call your visual descriptor profile.

  • 3. Designing your portrait:

    From your visual profile to a masterpiece

    Our studio creates fully abstract art pieces we call personality portraits. Over 40 descriptors inform the final look and feel of your art piece, from colours, composition, textures, to painting techniques. We start by talking through your snapshot results with you, as we share our initial thoughts on colour palette and general design.

  • 4. Portrait completion:

    Skillfully hand-crafted, truly unique

    Once the overall creative direction is greenlit, we paint! Our portraits are 100% human-made down to the canvas itself, and come with your visual profile and an in-depth analysis of your scores.

  • 5. Ready for pick-up or shipment!

    Carefully packaged, ready to hang

    All of our portraits are made on professional back-stapled exhibition canvases with an extra wide edge designed to eliminate framing requirements.
